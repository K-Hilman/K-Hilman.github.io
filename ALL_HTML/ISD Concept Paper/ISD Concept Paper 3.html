Needs Analysis

This interactive media is a form of lesson gamification.

Gamification provides positive effects on student's learning as said by (Juho et al.,2014).

The students will be more motivated to learn and will have the freedom to learn on their own term.

They  will also be able to know their efficacy of using English language by using the interactive media which includes a direct feedback upon answering a formative test.

A positive correlation  is found between self-efficacy in learning English language and student's achievement in the language among Malaysian High schools students as said by (Rahil et al,.2006)
